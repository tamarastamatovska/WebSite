WHAT IS SOFTWARE ARCHITECT?

A software architect is an individual who holds a multifaceted role within the realm of software development. Their primary responsibility lies in designing software solutions that effectively tackle the challenges and requirements faced by companies or clients. While traditional architects design physical structures, software architects focus on creating the blueprint and framework for software systems.The role of a software architect goes beyond just designing solutions. They also assume leadership responsibilities by overseeing a team of developers. This entails assigning and managing project tasks, engaging with clients to ensure that the proposed solution aligns with their needs and expectations, and providing regular updates on the progress of the project. Additionally, software architects play a crucial role in ensuring the smooth operation and coordination of all aspects of the software development process.
